Toucan is a non-profit organization working hard to provide free music lessons for less privileged children — so that cost is never the reason a child can't make music.

Sign up for lessons Volunteer with us

Music belongs to every child

Toucan was started by a group of friends who saw the cost of music education keep talented kids on the sidelines. We give underserved youth free lessons — and the joy, discipline, and community that come with learning an instrument.

What we do

Lessons that don't cost a thing

Every Toucan student is paired with a dedicated tutor for free, one-on-one lessons on the instrument they want to learn.

100% free lessons

No fees, ever. We exist so that money is never a barrier to a child learning music.

One-on-one tutoring

Personalized attention from a tutor who meets each student where they are and grows with them.

Piano, violin, viola & cello

Our tutors teach a range of instruments, so students can learn the one they're most excited about.
